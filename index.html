Our Judges

Teams will present their work at the end of the weekend to our panel of judges and the judges will award trophies for three categories: innovation, social impact, and business potential.

  • Criteria

Jackson Boyar

Shearwater is a company that partners with universities and secondary schools to provide transition support to new international and first-generation students. Shearwater's partners include Tufts University, Penn State, Northeastern, University of South Carolina, and over 30 of the best independent schools in the United States.

Tania Shev

Tania is an alumna of the Technology Innovation and Education program at HGSE. She is also the founder of the Ad Astra Group. The Ad Astra Group's mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment in order to see young professionals placed in careers they thrive in and love. She's also the founder of Career For Me, which works to develop effective solutions to challenges in communities by connecting young professionals and companies based on similar goals and visions.

Joanna Smith

AllHere, based across the river in the the Harvard Launch Lab, is an education organization whose mission is to engage the 6.5M+ families of children who miss 15+ days of a school a year, through data and family engagement initiatives. She has previous experience teaching and eighth grade reading and math. She also supported the launch of the U.S. Department of Education's Promise Neighborhood Initiative in Ward 7 of Washington, D.C. Her areas of expertise include customer development, customized learning, and comprehensive data assessment.
